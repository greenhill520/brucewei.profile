"And gladly would learn, and gladly teach."     --Chaucer

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer & Information Sciences, Temple University, supervised by Prof. Yu Wang. I also work closely with Prof. Zhu Han and Prof. Lei Fan in University of Houston.

My research interests include Edge Computing, Mobile Computing, Internet of Things, Federated Edge Learning, Reinforcement Learning at Edge, Quantum-based Network Optimization.

My current research focuses on resource management and federated learning in edge computing using either optimization or RL methods. I also focus on a new area in quantum computing, which is using the hybrid quantum-classical technique to optimize the communication network.
