"And gladly would learn, and gladly teach."     --Chaucer

Dr. Wei received his M.S. and B.E. degrees both in Software Engineering from SUN Yat-sen University, Guangzhou, China in 2016 and 2014, respectively. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer and Information Sciences from Temple University, Philadelphia, PA, USA in 2023. He is currently an Assistant Professor at Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (SIAT), Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is a member of IEEE and a member of CCF. He is a recipient of Outstanding Research Assistant Award from College of Science and Technology (2022) and Scott Hibbs Future of Computing Award from Department of Computer & Information Sciences (2023) at Temple University. His research interests include Edge Computing, Machine Learning, Quantum Algorithms and Applications. He is especially interested in resource management, edge network optimization, federated learning, reinforcement learning, quantum optimization algorithms, and quantum machine learning.
